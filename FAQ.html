Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying your next ride.

The Vehicles

Are the cars professionally inspected?

Yes! Every vehicle undergoes a rigorous 150-point safety inspection by our certified mechanics before being listed.

Can I see the vehicle history report?

Absolutely. We provide a free CARFAX or AutoCheck report for every car in our inventory directly on the listing page.

Do you offer warranties?

Most of our cars come with a 3-month/3,000-mile limited warranty. Extended protection plans are also available.

Financing

Do you offer in-house financing?

We work with over 20 different lenders to find the best rates for all credit types, including "Buy Here Pay Here" options.

How is my trade-in value calculated?

We use real-time market data from Kelley Blue Book and physical inspections to give you the most competitive trade-in value.

What documents do I need to bring?

You'll typically need your driver’s license, proof of insurance, and your two most recent pay stubs.

Delivery & Service

Do you deliver to my home?

Yes! We offer free home delivery within a 50-mile radius. Nationwide shipping is available for an additional fee.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day or 250-mile "No Questions Asked" money-back guarantee if you aren't completely satisfied.

Can I schedule a test drive online?

Yes, click the "Book Test Drive" button on any vehicle page to reserve your time slot instantly.
